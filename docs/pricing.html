Frequently Asked Questions

What are the limitations of the free trial?

We may change the limitations without any notifications in the future releases. The current ones are:

  • At most 1 saved connection
  • At most 1 database window
  • Alert mode is not available
  • Bookmark is not available
  • Command autocompleter is not available

Can I buy Medis for my team?

Of course! During the checkout process, you can select the quantity and enter the number of people for whom you are purchasing Medis. You will receive a separate license key for each person.
