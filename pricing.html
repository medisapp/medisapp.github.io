Frequently Asked Questions

What are the limitations of the free trial?

There is no time limit of the free trial, so you can use the trial as long as you want. However, there are several feature limits:

  • At most 2 connection favorites
  • Only a single window allowed
  • Key value filters are disabled
  • Alert mode is disabled
  • Bookmark is not available
  • Command autocompleter is not available
